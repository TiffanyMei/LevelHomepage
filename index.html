LEVEL

is a digital recruiting tool for HR/corporate recruiting that uses AI to reduce biases, specifically for women and underrepresented minorities. Our research suggests there is a need for large corporations to reduce bias in the LinkedIn search process and an interest to achieve these aims through blind hiring. However, current blind hiring solutions are limited and almost all offer no LinkedIn search functionality. This gap in the market represents a significant opportunity to sell a LinkedIn blind hiring tool to Fortune 500 companies with massive recruiting demands and mature diversity goals.
